Aaron Blutstein's Visual Design Final Project

I wanted to develop a small website specifically for children of between 3-6 years old where they could safely play online with a range of features without having to leave the website to look for similar activities in different locations. I looked towards my daughter for the type of activities I should develop, this included a painting application as she constantly attempts to try and draw with a computer mouse. I also developed other applications that would appeal more to older children around 5 years old as they learn to look for books and start typing, therefore the website needed to include such features that would keep this age group interested too. The theme of the overall website was focused around my daughter’s favourite soft toys - a rabbit that she calls ‘BABBIT, and an owl. By using soft toys in real-life scenarios it is the intention of the website to make the feel of the overall website more fun and appealing.
